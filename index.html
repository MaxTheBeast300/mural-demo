Middle Earth is full of different Architectural styles

The world of Middle Earth was carefully curated by talented artists and craftsman. 

Many miniatures were built referencing many styles from different periods like the Minas Tirith referencing Gothic architecture for the Cathedral standing on top the castle, and medieval style for the hill-like castle. 

Dwarves!

Dwarves have a particular architectural style that seem to originate from Art Nouveau. The philosophy behind their craft is sculpting the Earth. The stone is rough, so they sculpt it with straight, harsh lines, which reflects their characters, beings of harsh, unyielding nature.
